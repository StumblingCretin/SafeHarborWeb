Cybersecurity Consulting

Strategic expertise to build resilience.

Our virtual CISO (vCISO) services provide the strategic leadership you need to navigate the complex world of cybersecurity. We help you develop security roadmaps, create effective policies, and ensure you meet regulatory compliance standards.

OWASP & Secure Development: We integrate the OWASP Top 10 framework into your development lifecycle. Our experts provide guidance and testing to prevent common web application vulnerabilities, ensuring your code is secure from the ground up.

Open-Source Intelligence (OSINT): Knowledge is power. We use advanced OSINT techniques to discover what attackers can learn about you from public sources. This proactive approach helps identify potential attack vectors and exposed data before they can be exploited.

Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC): We help you navigate the complex web of industry regulations like ISO 27001, SOC 1, and SOC 2. Our experts work with you to build a comprehensive GRC framework that aligns security with business objectives, manages risk effectively, and ensures you're always audit-ready.

PCI DSS Compliance: For businesses that handle cardholder data, achieving and maintaining PCI DSS compliance is non-negotiable. We provide end-to-end guidance, from gap analysis and remediation to helping you prepare for your Report on Compliance (ROC).

Your employees are your first line of defense. Our SATE programs go beyond standard training modules, using engaging, interactive content and realistic phishing simulations to empower your team. We turn your staff from potential liabilities into a proactive, security-conscious workforce, significantly reducing the risk of human error.

  • Security Program & Strategy Development
  • GRC, ISO 27001 & SOC 2 Consulting
  • PCI DSS Compliance Consulting
  • OWASP & Secure SDLC Guidance
  • Open-Source Intelligence (OSINT) Analysis
  • Security Awareness Training & Education (SATE)
